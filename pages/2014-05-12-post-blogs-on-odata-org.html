If you haven't got an OData.org contributor accountYou will need an OData.org contributor account to post blogs. To get that, you should fill in the forms. After your request is reviewed and approved, you will receive an email of account information with your username and initial password. Please remember to log in at site admin page to change your initial password.

If you have a blog post pending publishingPlease note that contributors can only write and save their posts but not directly publish them. If you have a blog post pending publish, you can notify us by filling in the form. After your blog is reviewed and published, you will receive a confirmation mail.
