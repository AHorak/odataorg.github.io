Acumatica

Acumatica, founded in 2008, is a technology provider that develops adaptable cloud- and browser-based enterprise resource planning (ERP) software designed to improve productivity of small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs). The company is headquartered in the Seattle metropolitan area and has a number of satellite offices around the globe. Business data and lists created in generic inquiries can be exposed as OData endpoints.
