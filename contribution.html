Contribute to OData.org

Post blogs: Blogs on OData.org are for OData lovers to share their experiances of OData. If you want to promote your work (related with OData), as long as it's not overt-selling , send a pull request following the contribution guidelines. After a light review process, we will publish your blog.

Other improvements: OData.org is always open to any kind of contribution, including adding contents to Ecosystem and Libraries. Please visit OData.org repo on GitHub for more details. Or if you have any further questions / feedback, please create GitHub issues or send email to odata-discussion@googlegroups.com.



Participate elsewhere

OASIS community: You can view and create issues related to OData protocol directy at OASIS OData TC.

Mailing list: For any question related to OData, please join the OData Discussion Group. For questions related to specific stacks, you can also send email directly to :

  • Microsoft OData Team: odatafeedback@microsoft.com
  • Apache Olingo: Apache Olingo Support

Twitter: Follow us (@odataorg) on Twitter and tweets #odata

StackOverflow: Ask questions on with OData tag StackOverflow
